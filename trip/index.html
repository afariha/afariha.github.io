Tale of a Cross-Country Road Trip

I always wanted to go for a cross-country road trip, from the east coast to the west coast. East coast has its own charm, it has 4 seasons: Summer, Fall, Spring, and Winter... but for some mysterious reasons, I always felt a very strong connection with the west coast, specially Washington. Finally, my long-time dream is coming true as I got a job at Washington! I am going to join Microsoft this summer and I am very excited about it. It's now time to leave Massachusetts for good and move to my favorite state, Washington. Yay! I am counting days until the trip actually starts. I will write about my journey here. Stay tuned!
