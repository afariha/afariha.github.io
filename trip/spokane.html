From Pioneer Valley to Puget Sound

Evergreen State

I entered Washington today, the Evergreen State. I crossed two state borders, Idaho and Washington. Also, I am now in the Pacific Time Zone, lagging 3 hours from where I started. This completes my MA to WA trip, now I need to complete Sunderland to Redmond part. The road today was super curvy as it crosses several mountains, and I was having slight motion sickness due to such frequent turns, even though it is uncommon for drivers to experience motion sickness. The landscape was pretty all the way. I passed Coeur d'Alene, a famous city in Idaho on my way. I got glimpses of Coeur d'Alene lake, it seemed pretty. After three days of inability to access Uber Eats in South Dakota, Wyoming, and Montana, today I was able to order via Uber Eats again. Looks like I am back to civilization!
