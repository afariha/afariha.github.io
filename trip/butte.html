From Pioneer Valley to Puget Sound

Big Sky Country

Montana is known as Big Sky Country. I had so much fun during my trip today that it is hard to express in writing. I strongly recommend watching the above time-lapse video. The sky was indeed big and showed me many different colors. It even snowed for a while, rained too. The landscape was spectacular. Today, for the first time I spotted a few cars from Washington, looks like I am closer to my new home. I absolutely loved the amazing mountains covered with snow all around me almost the entire time.

Near Park City, Montana, I got this amazing view from I-90. This place is also featured in I-90's wikipedia page.

At some point, I saw a clean border separating thick dark cloud and clear sky.

Butte is a pretty mountain town, roads are hilly.

I achieved a unique milestone today, my car's odometer now reads 44444!

Today's airbnb was a studio, nice and cozy. There was no door between the rooms which I found a bit odd!
