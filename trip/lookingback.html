From Pioneer Valley to Puget Sound

Retrospect

In total, I drove about 3500+ miles in 10 days with a total driving time of about 60 hours. The days were very diverse, full of surprises. The weather never failed to surprise me. I saw rain, cold, sun, snow, mist, and wind. I saw river, falls, lakes, mountains, sky, tree, people, and animals. I ate a diverse types of food and lived in different kinds of home in airbnb. I passed many toll plazas, visited many rest areas, and gas stations. Some of the food I bought from gas stations, I loved them, but I don't know where to find them again. I changed lanes a thousand times, passed thousands of cars and trucks. After 10 days of morning routine of pack your things, load your car, and keep driving, I am feeling stuck today, a little bit of goalless as well. I don't have anywhere else to go any more, all I need to do is just stay. The trip was a bit tiring, but would I repeat it again? hell yes!
